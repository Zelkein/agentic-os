## What Is This?

You now have three AI agents that can help with your MEP work:

### 🧑‍🏫 Sensei (Coach)
**What it does:** Reviews your work, identifies errors, teaches best practices
**Ask it:**
- "Review my HVAC calculation"
- "Is my electrical load sizing correct?"
- "How should I handle this coordination conflict?"

### 🤖 Jasper (Orchestrator)
**What it does:** Coordinates MEP workflows, manages priorities, explains processes
**Ask it:**
- "What's our MEP process for this project?"
- "We have coordination issues. What should we do?"
- "How should we organize the calculation phase?"

### 👥 Personal Assistant (Coming in next phase)
**What it does:** General support, calculations, quick answers
**Ask it:**
- "Help me organize the calculations"
- "What standards apply here?"
- "Explain what you need from me"

## How to Use

### The Simple Way

1. **Type a question in the chat** about MEP work
2. **The system analyzes your message** and routes to the right agent
3. **The agent responds** with advice, review, or guidance
4. **You keep the conversation going** if you need follow-up questions

### Examples

#### Sensei Review

System: Recognizes this is a review task

```

#### Jasper Coordination
```

↓

↓

## Best Practices

### 1. Be Specific
❌ "Review this"
✅ "Review my HVAC load calculation for 14-unit project"

### 2. Include Context
❌ "Is this right?"
✅ "I calculated peak cooling load at 150 kW for a 5-story building. Is the chiller sizing correct?"

### 3. Ask Follow-ups
The agents remember your conversation:
```
You: "What's the process for electrical load calculation?"
Agent: "First, determine connected load..."
You: "How do we handle diversity factors?"
Agent: "Good question. For residential..."
```

### 4. Accept Feedback Constructively
Sensei gives specific feedback. Ask "why" if you don't understand:
```
Sensei: "Your chiller is undersized by 15%"
You: "Why? I included a 10% safety margin"
Sensei: "For humidity control, you need 15% minimum. Here's why..."
```

## Common Questions

### Q: Is the agent replacing me?
**A:** No. Sensei helps you do better work. You still make the decisions.

### Q: What about confidential project info?
**A:** The agents see what you type. Don't share sensitive client info or passwords.

### Q: Will my questions be logged?
**A:** Yes. We track responses to improve the system. All data stays internal.

### Q: What if the agent gets it wrong?
**A:** That's exactly what we're testing. Tell us what was wrong so we can fix it.

### Q: Can I use this for projects I'm on?
**A:** During testing, only for validation. After approval, yes—you'll use it for real work.

### Q: How fast does it respond?
**A:** Typically 5-15 seconds. Sometimes longer if thinking hard.

### Q: Does it know about our projects?
**A:** It knows about CMI, our team structure, and MEP process. Ask it about your project.
